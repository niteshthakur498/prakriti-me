# Project PRD: PrakritiMe

## 1. Executive Summary
**PrakritiMe** is a warm, lively, and uplifting wellness application designed to help users discover their unique Ayurvedic mind-body constitution (*Prakriti*). It bridges ancient Vedic wisdom with modern interface design, offering a "sunlit garden" experience that is energetic, trustworthy, and accessible.

## 2. Brand Identity
*   **Name:** PrakritiMe
*   **Tagline:** "Discover your nature. Live in harmony."
*   **Value Proposition:** Find your Ayurvedic body type in 5 minutes to guide a life in perfect balance.
*   **Core Personality:** Warm, earthy, modern, and culturally rich. Not clinical, not overly spiritual—rooted in wellness pride.

## 3. Visual Language (Vedic Vitality System)
### Color Palette
*   **Primary:** Saffron (#F4A023 - Energy/CTA), Forest Green (#2D6A4F - Balance), Cream (#FDF6EC - Surface).
*   **Dosha Specifics:**
    *   **Vata:** Sky Blue + Silver (#7EC8E3)
    *   **Pitta:** Coral + Flame Gold (#FF6B6B)
    *   **Kapha:** Forest + Earth Brown (#52796F)
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headings:** *Playfair Display* (Serif) for authority and warmth.
    *   **Body:** *Inter* (Sans-serif) for modern clarity.
    *   **Authenticity:** *Noto Serif Devanagari* for Sanskrit terminology.

## 4. User Journey & Feature Set
### Phase 1: Discovery (MVP)
1.  **Immersive Landing Page:** High-impact hero section with trust badges and a clear path to the assessment.
2.  **Dosha Quiz:** A 25-question interactive experience with section-specific color shifts (Body, Mind, Digestion, etc.) and joyful micro-interactions.
3.  **Personalized Results:** Dynamic breakdown of constitution percentages (e.g., Vata-Pitta) with actionable recommendations for:
    *   **Diet:** Favor vs. Avoid foods.
    *   **Routine:** Ideal daily timeline (*Dinacharya*).
    *   **Yoga:** Dosha-balancing poses with Sanskrit names.
4.  **Educational Hub:** Long-form "About Prakriti" content with clinical depth toggles for practitioners.

### Phase 2: Engagement (V2 Hooks)
*   **Vedic AI Assistant:** Chatbot for personalized Ayurvedic advice.
*   **Practitioner Mode:** Specialized views and raw data scores for wellness professionals.
*   **Patient Profiles:** Secure saving and tracking of constitution history.
*   **Multilingual Support:** Expanded locales (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu).

## 5. Technical Requirements
*   **Performance:** Fast-loading, single-page transitions (300ms target).
*   **Accessibility:** WCAG AA compliance, support for `prefers-reduced-motion`.
*   **Mobile-First:** Optimized for high-performance mobile browsing (primary target audience).
*   **Animation:** Use of CSS transforms and scale for interactive feedback (200ms ease).

## 6. Design Principles
*   **Fidelity to Tradition:** Reproduce Sanskrit terms character-for-character.
*   **Visual Warmth:** Avoid cold grays; use tinted shadows and cream surfaces.
*   **Inclusive Representation:** Illustrations representing diverse human silhouettes.

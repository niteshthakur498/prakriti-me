What is Prakriti?

Prakriti is your unique energetic blueprint—the specific combination of physical, mental, and emotional characteristics determined at the moment of your conception. It is the "nature" in your human nature.

"स्वस्थस्य स्वास्थ्यरक्षणं आतुरस्य विकारप्रशमनं च।"

"To protect the health of the healthy and to alleviate the disorders of the diseased."

Charaka Samhita, Sutra Sthana

The Three Pillars: Doshas

Ayurveda categorizes these energies into three functional principles, or Doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. While everyone has all three, their proportions create your distinct Prakriti.

Vata

air

The energy of movement. Governed by Air and Ether. When in balance, it fosters creativity and flexibility.

Clinical Depth (For Practitioners) expand_more

Sub-doshas: Prana, Udana, Samana, Vyana, Apana.

Qualities: Laghu (Light), Sheeta (Cold), Rooksha (Dry), Khara (Rough).

Primary Site: Colon (Pakwashaya).

Pitta

local_fire_department

The energy of transformation. Governed by Fire and Water. Responsible for digestion, metabolism, and intelligence.

Clinical Depth (For Practitioners) expand_more

Sub-doshas: Pachaka, Ranjaka, Sadhaka, Alochaka, Bhrajaka.

Qualities: Sasneha (Slightly oily), Teekshna (Sharp), Ushna (Hot).

Primary Site: Small Intestine (Amashaya).

Kapha

water_drop

The energy of lubrication and structure. Governed by Earth and Water. Provides stamina, calmness, and immunity.

Clinical Depth (For Practitioners) expand_more

Sub-doshas: Kledaka, Avalambaka, Bodhaka, Tarpaka, Shleshaka.

Qualities: Guru (Heavy), Manda (Slow), Sthira (Stable), Snigdha (Unctuous).

Primary Site: Chest (Uras).

The Three Doshas

Everything in the universe is a unique combination of five elements, manifesting in us as three life forces.

Vata

Air & Space

Creative, light-hearted, and energetic when in balance. Like the wind, Vata is always moving.

🔥 पित्त (Pitta)

Pitta

Kapha

  • circle Stability

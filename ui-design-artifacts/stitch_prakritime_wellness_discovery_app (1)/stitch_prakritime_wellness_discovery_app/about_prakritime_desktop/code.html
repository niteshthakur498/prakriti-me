Understanding Prakriti

Prakriti is your unique biological blueprint—the specific combination of physical, mental, and emotional characteristics that define your true nature.

The Concept of Individual Constitution

Derived from the Sanskrit words Pra (original) and Kriti (creation), Prakriti is established at the moment of conception. While our bodies change over time, this underlying foundation remains constant, serving as a compass for our health choices.

"देहसत्त्वविशेषाणां प्रकृतिः साभिधीयते ॥"

"The specific distinctiveness of the body and mind is called Prakriti." — Charaka Samhita

Genetic Factors

Modern research parallels Prakriti with phenotypic expressions and genomic variations. Each constitution dictates metabolic rates (Agni) and tissue quality (Dhatu Saar).
